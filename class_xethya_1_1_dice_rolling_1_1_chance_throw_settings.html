Xethya.DiceRolling.ChanceThrowSettings.ChanceThrowSettings ( )

Instantiates the default configuration for a chance throw. Thus, a roll of 1 thru 20 will be of Failure; a roll of 21 thru 90 will be of Success; and a roll of 91 thru 100 will be of Critical Success.
